The shape of it: the player sits at a real 3-seat table — You = the Bandit, Sir Aldric = the Knight, Old Wren = the Fisherman — and plays a genuine Act 1 into the start of Act 2. Key cards are swapped into the hand at the right beats (always from the correct act's own deck), the mission pool is arranged so Helping the Merchant is available, and everything else — throws, reveals, the pass-left draft, the missions phase, the Melee cinematic — is the real game. While a prompt is up, a shield blocks every tap except the one thing the step allows.
